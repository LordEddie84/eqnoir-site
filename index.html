ABOUT EQNOIR

EQNOIR is a futuristic digital reserve ecosystem designed to empower communities through innovation, financial sovereignty, and generational wealth creation. Built on the BNB Smart Chain, EQNOIR integrates decentralized finance, digital marketplaces, and ecosystem reserves supporting housing, food access, legal support, and technological advancement. The mission is to build one of the most innovative community-driven digital reserves in the world.
